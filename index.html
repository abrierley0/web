Jose Ignacio Blanco Garcia-Valdecasas

Bass Singer

About

Jose has performed in concerts across Granada, Spain, building an impressive classical and popular repertoire.

As part of El Coro De Granada, he rehearses at the Manuel De Falla Auditorium. Last September, Jose sang at the La Plaza de Toros de Granada as part of a 'Grand Choirs of the Opera' concert, featuring classic operatic excerpts such as Il Trovatore's Anvil Chorus.

Jose's solo career is growing fast. He recently featured as a soloist in Greece the musical and at the Stadium de Granada.
