Jose Ignacio Blanco Garcia-Valdecasas

Bass Singer

About

Jose Blanco has performed in concerts across Granada, Spain, building an impressive classical and popular repertoire.

As part of El Joven Coro de la Orquesta Ciudad de Granada (JCOCG), he rehearses at the Manuel De Falla Auditorium. Last September, Jose sang with the JCOCG at the La Plaza de Toros de Granada as part of a 'Grand Choirs of the Opera' concert, featuring classic operatic excerpts such as Il Trovatore's Anvil Chorus.

Jose also performs as part of El Coro Ciudad de Granada. Recent performances include his solo role to a large audience Nuevo Los Cármenes, a large football stadium in Granada.

Jose's solo career is growing fast. He recently featured as a soloist in 'Grease' the musical for the JCOCG, rehearsing at Caja Rural Auditorium.
