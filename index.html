About

Jose Blanco has performed in concerts across Granada, Spain, building an impressive classical and popular repertoire.

As part of El Joven Coro de la Orquesta Ciudad de Granada (JCOCG), he rehearses at the Auditorio Manuel de Falla. Last September, Jose sang at the La Plaza de Toros de Granada as part of a 'Grand Choirs of the Opera' concert, featuring classic operatic excerpts such as Il Trovatore's Anvil Chorus.

Jose's solo career is growing fast. He recently featured as a soloist in 'Grease' the musical for the JCOCG, rehearsing at Auditorio Caja Rural.
